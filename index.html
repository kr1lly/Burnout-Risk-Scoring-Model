Scoring method

How each observation is calculated

01

Average the four responses in each variable.

02

Reverse protective scores: 5 − sleep and 5 − coping.

03

Apply weights: 30% stress, 30% workload, 20% sleep risk, and 20% coping risk.

BRSM: 0.30(academic stress) + 0.30(academic workload) + 0.20(sleep risk) + 0.20(coping risk)
